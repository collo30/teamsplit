Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Introduction

TeamSplit ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our team allocation tool at this website.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Team Member Names: Names you enter are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and are never transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use our site.
  • Cookies: Third-party advertising partners may use cookies to serve personalized ads.

Local Storage

TeamSplit uses your browser's localStorage to save your member list and preferences. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser data

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your locally stored data at any time via browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Use browser extensions to block tracking

Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit personal information, there is minimal security risk associated with using TeamSplit.

Children's Privacy

TeamSplit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
